Key Features

Search Functionality

This feature allows users (students, teachers, and librarians) to search for books or resources within the library collection. Users can search by title, author, subject, or keyword to locate the material they need.

Cataloging

Cataloging involves organizing and classifying library materials systematically. It includes creating detailed records for each item, including information like title, author, genre, and publication date.

Research Management

This feature helps users manage their research projects effectively. It includes tools for organizing research material, such as citations, notes, and sources. It also provides access to journals and other resources that can support research activities.

For: Students, Teachers, Researchers & Community
